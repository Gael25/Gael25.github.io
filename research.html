Working Papers

Ethnicity and Place in African Opinions

with Etienne Le Rossignol (University of Namur)
Working paper, 2026 · Presented at research workshop

Using Afrobarometer data matched with an ethnographic atlas, this paper quantifies the extent to which individual variation in attitudes, opinions, and political preferences is explained by ethnic affiliation versus region of residence.

Work in Progress

Carbon Concessions and Socioeconomic Outcomes in Africa

Ongoing, 2026

Analyzing the effect of establishing carbon concessions on local socioeconomic outcomes in Africa. Building a spatial database from multiple sources, converting shapefiles into cell-based rasters, and conducting event study analyses on nighttime lights and political outcomes.

Term after Term: The Dynamic Path of Incumbency in French Municipal Elections

Master's thesis, 2024

Applying political RDD to analyze the incumbency advantage dynamics over time in French municipal elections. Investigating the relationship between incumbency effects and municipal budget management. Built a database of French mayors by web scraping multiple municipal sources.
